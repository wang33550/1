# Code of Conduct

## Our Standard

Be direct, respectful, and technically honest.

Expected behavior:

- discuss problems, not people
- explain tradeoffs concretely
- accept correction quickly
- keep collaboration focused on the work

Unacceptable behavior:

- harassment
- personal attacks
- deliberate misinformation
- hostile gatekeeping

Project maintainers may moderate discussions and contributions to preserve a productive technical environment.
